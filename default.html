    Use case: Wearables for worker safety

    Business problem:
    How do we know what covered employees actually do in the workplace?
    How do we manage workers’ compensation exposure risk more precisely and with more frequent audits?
    So what's the business opportunity? >
    Business opportunity:
    Dynamic workplace safety programs – enabled by data from wearables and employee portals
    Automated, weekly premium audits – enabled by robots
    Integrated and aggregated worker safety scoring and incentives – enabled by gamification
    Now, see how it works >
    Anthony
    Construction Worker
    • Anthony clocks in and is handed a safety vest connected with wearable sensors. He puts on the vest and is assigned to his first job for the day.
    • Anthony’s assignment is to install ceiling tiles.
    See what happens next >
    Benny
    Robo Saftey Advisor
    • Benny, the robo safety advisor that is programmed to read Anthony’s sensor data and compare movements to benchmarks, collects data for 60 minutes.
    • Benny sends Anthony a message that he should take a five-minute break.
    See what happens next >
    Sensors
    • During Anthony’s break his safety vest uploads sensor data to his employer’s smart policy.
    See what happens next >
    Anthony
    Construction Worker
    • Anthony continues working with Benny alerting him throughout the day when it is time for breaks.
    • Anthony’s safety score is calculated. It is comparably high and he is contracted for longer- term assignments.
    See what it takes >

    What makes it possible

    Telematics and sensor-enabled devices and wearables will capture risk data generated through people, equipment, material and the environment.

    Dynamic worker safety data is aggregated within the risk-assessment process and used by underwriters to monitor and quantify risk in real-time.

    Employer gathers all the real-time data and can take actionable insights – effectively reducing injury rate while partnering more closely with carrier to improve risk management.

    Core Insure processing platform manages policy and billing transactions with leveraged robotics automation and comprehensive portfolio underwriting.
