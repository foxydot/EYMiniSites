So what's the business opportunity? >
Business opportunity:
Insurers have the data they need to identify likely customers more efficiently and reach out to them in automated yet personalized ways

Mary
Policy underwriter

Texts his client Anne (who also prefers text communications)
Anne
Client
  • Receives and reviews the offer

  • Texts Rick the news – “Let’s talk soon”
See what happens next >
Rick gets notified!
Rick
Agent
  • Receives automated notification
  • Decides to review other offers from Mary

Use Case: Intelligent Cross Selling

Business problem:
Insurers lack insight into events in customers’ lives that would represent upsell and cross-sell opportunities, as well as the capabilities to efficiently and effectively develop and distribute targeted offers.
So what's the business opportunity? >
Business opportunity:
Artificial intelligence, when combined with mobile portals, enables insurers to engage customers renewing their policies and generate more revenue through targeted and timely offers for additional coverage.
Now, see how it works >
John
Insured
  • Holds bundled household policy with ABC Insurance Co.
  • Has a son, Dan, just graduating and moving back to the family neighborhood.
See what happens next >
Dan
Son of John
  • Successfully negotiates with John to add his car to the family policy
See what happens next >
John
Insured
  • Receives an alert from ABC app congratulating him on Dan’s recent graduation
  • Connects to the App to add Dan’s car to bundled policy
See what happens next >
Abe
ABC's AI Bot
  • Monitors policy change and determines that Dan is moving into an apartment
  • Automatically configures a renter’s policy and presents John with a bundled renter’s policy offer
See what happens next >
John
Insured
  • Likes Abe’s idea and clicks to see the bundled savings, which are in line with his expectations
  • Buys the renters policy
See what happens next >
Abe
ABC's AI Bot
  • Automatically completes all transactions
  • Issues the updated policies
  • Notifies portfolio underwriter who manages John’s bundled home account that all policies have been issued
See what it takes >
